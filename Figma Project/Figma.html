Clay Target Shooting is a sport that can be enjoyed by the whole family as a recreation at a local gun club, or at a competition level.

Clay target shooting has different disciplines to try which include: Trap, Skeet, ISSF and Sporting Clays. Each discipline varies in its approach and delivery targets to give a choice and different experience with Clay Target Shooting.

Sports: CTS(Clay Target Shooting)

The website is an information site for people who may be interested to the sport or people don’t know about it a lot. All given information can be found on other sites within the internet.
